Economy Macros

Module

Manage user balances, bank accounts, and transactions.

s.cash

s.cash[User?]

Returns the cash balance of a user. Defaults to command author if no ID provided.

s.reply[You have: s.cash]

s.bank

s.bank[User?]

Returns the bank balance of a user.

s.reply[Bank Balance: s.bank]

s.addCash

s.addCash[Amount; User?]

Adds cash to a user. Supports negative values.

s.addCash[100; s.authorId]

s.pay

s.pay[Amount; UserID]

Transfer money from your wallet to another user.

s.pay[50; s.arg[1]]

s.deposit

s.deposit[Amount or 'all']

Move money from Cash to Bank.

s.deposit[all]

s.withdraw

s.withdraw[Amount or 'all']

Move money from Bank to Cash.

s.withdraw[500]
